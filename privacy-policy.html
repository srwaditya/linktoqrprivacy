üîí Privacy Policy Overview

LINKTOQR is a Chrome extension designed with privacy and user protection as core principles. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Last Updated: February 2026

Our Commitment: We believe privacy is fundamental. LINKTOQR collects minimal data necessary only for its core functionality. All data is stored locally on your device.

üìä Data Collection Philosophy

LINKTOQR follows a privacy-first approach with minimal data collection:

  • Minimal Collection: We only collect data essential for core features
  • Local Storage: All data stays on your device
  • No Transmission: Data is never sent to external servers
  • User Control: You control all your data

‚úÖ What We Collect

User Preferences

QR code customization settings such as:

  • Theme preferences (light/dark mode)
  • Default QR code size and format
  • Error correction level preferences

QR Code History

A local history of QR codes you've generated, stored on your device. This allows you to:

  • Access previously generated QR codes
  • Re-generate codes without re-entering data
  • Manage your QR code library

URLs Converted

The URLs and text you convert to QR codes are stored locally for your convenience and history tracking.

Premium Account Status

Information about whether you have a premium subscription to unlock advanced features.

üíæ How We Store Your Data

Local Storage Only

All LINKTOQR data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your computer
  • No external servers have access to your information
  • You have complete control over your data

Chrome Sync (Optional)

If you have Chrome sync enabled, premium account status may be synced across your Chrome devices for continuity. This data is encrypted by Google and follows Google's privacy policies.

Data Deletion

You can delete all LINKTOQR data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the extension
  • Using the clear data option in extension settings
  • Clearing browser storage for the extension

üîë Permission Justifications

activeTab

Purpose: Access the currently active tab to generate QR codes

Why needed: Required to read the URL of the current webpage so we can generate a QR code representing that URL. This permission only applies to tabs you explicitly click on.

scripting

Purpose: Execute scripts in web pages when needed

Why needed: Allows the extension to inject QR code functionality and interact with page content for enhanced features.

storage

Purpose: Store user data persistently

Why needed: Enables saving user preferences, QR code history, premium status, and other settings across browser sessions.

clipboardWrite

Purpose: Copy generated QR codes to clipboard

Why needed: Allows you to quickly copy QR codes for pasting into other applications.

download

Purpose: Download generated QR codes

Why needed: Enables downloading QR codes as image files for sharing, printing, or archival purposes.

host_permissions (<all_urls>)

Purpose: Access all websites

Why needed: Allows the extension to generate QR codes from any URL you visit, providing seamless functionality across all web pages.
